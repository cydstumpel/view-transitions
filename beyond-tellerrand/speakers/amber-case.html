Designing Calm Technology

Amber Case studies the interactions between humans and technology, and how technology affects culture. She is an internationally recognized design advocate and speaker, and the author of four books, including Calm Technology and A Kids Book About Technology. She spent two years as a fellow at MIT's Center for Civic Media and Harvard's Berkman Klein Center for Internet & Society and was a 2021 Mozilla Fellow.
