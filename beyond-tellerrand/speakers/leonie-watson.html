Léonie Watson

There Is No Spoon

Léonie Watson is a Director of TetraLogical, a company that helps organizations create inclusive digital products and services. She is a member of the W3C Advisory Board and co-chair of the W3C Web Applications Working Group. Léonie is a passionate advocate for accessibility and inclusive design, and she regularly speaks at conferences worldwide.
