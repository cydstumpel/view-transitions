Gavin Strange

Creative Side Projects

Gavin Strange is a designer, animator, and director at Aardman Animations. He is known for his creative side projects and personal work, which he shares under the name JamFactory. Gavin is passionate about creativity, making things, and helping others find their creative voice. He is the author of "Do Fly" and "Make Your Own Luck."
