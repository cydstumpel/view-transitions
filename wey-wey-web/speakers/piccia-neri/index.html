Piccia Neri

Accessible Design Trainer & Consultant

Piccia Neri is on a mission to prove that inclusive design doesn't kill creativity: it fuels it. As a UX and accessible design consultant, she leads global projects for companies of all sizes and trains designers, marketers and developers to put accessibility at the heart of their work. Piccia speaks at conferences worldwide, and her current main focus is on balancing creativity and accessibility, without sacrificing either.
